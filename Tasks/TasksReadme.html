Steps to Run Webpage

  1. Activate Virtual Environment

  1. Install Requirements for app.py

  1. Run app.py, and move to link http://127.0.0.1:5000/ on the web-page

  1. The Home Page of the Website is loaded

  1. Move to Login window to access the portal

After Login, based on your login credentials you will be redirected to:
